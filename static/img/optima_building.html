OPTIMA SIGNATURE® | DOWNTOWN CHICAGO APARTMENTS

A Modern-Day Masterpiece

Welcome to Optima Signature® , a beautiful residential apartment community located in Streeterville, Chicago. At 57 stories tall, this LEED Certified building stands out against the historic skyline. Inside you’ll find a building that “wows” you with its finishes, features, and amenities—all carefully chosen to craft a modern lifestyle. With 1.5 acres of community amenity space spread across four floors, you’ll have everything you need at your fingertips. Resort-style luxuries include indoor and outdoor heated swimming pools, cabanas, and bars. Experience a cutting-edge fitness center that offers a WELLBEATS™ virtual workout studio and steam rooms. Come experience the modern lifestyle that Optima Signature® can provide. These downtown Chicago apartments have everything you need for the ultimate living experience.

Whether you’re looking for a studio, convertible, 1-, 2-, or 3-bedroom apartment home, Optima Signature® has an option that will suit you. Layouts are designed using an open floor concept that combines the great room (living and dining room) and kitchen, feature modern, luxury finishes, and offer incredible city and lake views.

Live Luxuriously at Optima Signature® Downtown Apartments Chicago

A Signature Collection

Complementing our unmatched selection of downtown Chicago apartments, Optima Signature® offers modern in-apartment finishes and luxury community amenities that deliver endless options to enrich your daily life.

Homes have been tailored to best suit a modern lifestyle offering features like built-in stainless steel appliances, quartz or granite bathroom counters, Snaidero® cabinetry, Balterio® wide-plank flooring, and two selections of materials and finishes.

At Optima Signature® residents have unlimited access to exclusive community amenities across three floors Experience a one-of-a-kind environment with resort-style luxuries, athletic facilities, and more. In the elite athletic spaces, practice your golf on the putting green or golf simulator. Spend time with your kids at the indoor and outdoor play spaces, and de-stress at the end of the day at the yoga studio or in the on-site saunas and steam rooms. Optima Signature® also offers an array of services to make life more convenient, including in-home package delivery, room service, housekeeping, bike storage and repair, car wash and detailing, fitness classes and personal training.
